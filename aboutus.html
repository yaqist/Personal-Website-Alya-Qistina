Our Story

Est. 2023 — Pantai Kemayang, Bachok

Hattori Coffee is a cafe that opened in March 2023 at Pantai Kemayang, Bachok Kelantan. While delivering good quality coffees and foods, we also want to create good memories for the customer while enjoying the view of the sea. Come and heal your inner self!

Located right by the beach, Hattori Coffee combines high-quality beverages with a laid-back coastal vibe that is hard to find elsewhere — making it an ideal destination for the Bachok community and anyone looking for a great hangout spot in Kelantan.

More than just coffee, Hattori Coffee also offers a variety of beverages including matcha and non-coffee options, along with Western dishes that are perfect for casual dining with family and friends.

Our Vision

To become a beloved coffee destination in Bachok by offering quality beverages, a welcoming atmosphere, and memorable experiences that bring people together.

Our Mission

  • To serve high-quality coffee and beverages made with care.
  • To create a comfortable and relaxing space for everyone.
  • To offer a diverse menu catering to different tastes.
  • To provide friendly service that makes you feel valued.
  • To contribute positively to the Bachok community.
